About Us


Rolls-Royce was a British luxury car and later an aero-engine manufacturing
business established in 1904 in Manchester, United Kingdom by the partnership
of Charles Rolls and Henry Royce. Building on Royce's reputation established
with his cranes they quickly developed a reputation for superior engineering
by manufacturing the "best car in the world". The First World War brought them
into manufacturing aero-engines. Joint development of jet engines began in
1940 and they entered production. Rolls-Royce has built an enduring reputation for development and manufacture of engines for defence and
civil aircraft.
